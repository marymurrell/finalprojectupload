Prompt: A Coloured Headshot

Introduction

Artificial intelligence plays a noticeable role in shaping our perspectives, influencing our decisions and constructing societal norms, but beneath the surface of this technology lie hidden biases which are dangerous to systemic inequalities. These image-generating algorithms perpetuate harmful racial stereotypes which are outdated.

Algorithmic bias occurs when the model makes a decision which disadvantages particular social groups and that starts with the data that the technology is trained with, meaning that humans are still enforcing racial stereotypes. Without diverse representation in the data, AI systems cannot accurately reflect the complexities of the human experience.

In the context of Stable Diffusion, this project will seek to creatively display the hidden data beneath these images, such as the RGB values to construct the skintones to show the vast contrast of associations with various topics and skin colour, and to expose bias present within the model.

Each image was generated using Stable Diffusion, an AI image generator. The prompt entered was “A coloured headshot of…”, taking a topic as an input. An average RGB value of each image was generated by taking data from five different facial points, forehead, nose, left cheek, right cheek and chin. Hover over each image to the left to view this effect.

When taking away all other elements, it leaves no choice but to focus on the skin tone and reflect how this differs across various prompts. Use the left and right arrows on the screen or on your keyboard to navigate through this digital gallery.
